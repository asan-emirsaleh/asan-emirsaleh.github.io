## Overview

This dataset contains a curated sample list of short audiorecords illustrating non-standard phonological phenomena in Crimean Tatar dialects from the Sudak (Sudaq) region, Crimea. The recording was performed by Asan Emirsale, Girey Bair, Refat Izzet, Enver Mecit, during 2009-2026. The recordings deposited to video hosting platforms were also used.
